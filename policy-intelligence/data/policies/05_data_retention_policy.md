# Data Retention Policy

## 1. Purpose
Defines minimum retention periods for corporate records to satisfy legal and regulatory obligations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to financial records, transaction logs, and business correspondence.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Financial Records
Financial and transactional records must be retained for a minimum of 7 years from the date of creation, in accordance with SOX requirements.

### 3.2 Audit Logs
Security and access audit logs must be retained for a minimum of 3 years.

### 3.3 Disposal
Records must be securely destroyed only after the applicable retention period has elapsed and only with sign-off from Legal.
