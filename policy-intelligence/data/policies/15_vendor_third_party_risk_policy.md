# Vendor & Third-Party Risk Policy

## 1. Purpose
Defines security due diligence requirements for third-party vendors.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all vendors with access to corporate systems or data.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Security Assessment
Vendors must complete a security questionnaire aligned to NIST SP 800-53 Rev 4 prior to onboarding.

### 3.2 Contractual Requirements
Vendor contracts must include a right-to-audit clause and breach notification requirements within 72 hours.

### 3.3 Annual Review
Vendor security posture must be reassessed annually for vendors with access to Confidential or Restricted data.
