# Identity & Access Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
Defines identity, authentication, and privileged access requirements.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all corporate identity systems, including privileged and administrative accounts.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 MFA for Privileged Accounts
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) must be enforced for all privileged and administrative accounts on all systems, with no exceptions.

### 3.2 Least Privilege
Access must be granted according to least-privilege principles and reviewed quarterly.

### 3.3 Deprovisioning
Access must be revoked within 24 hours of employee termination.
