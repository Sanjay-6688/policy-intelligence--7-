# Data Classification Policy

## 1. Purpose
Establishes classification tiers and associated protection requirements for corporate data.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all data created, processed, or stored by the organization.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Classification Tiers
Data must be classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.

### 3.2 Encryption by Tier
Confidential and Restricted data must be encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (minimum AES-256) and in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.

### 3.3 Access Control
Access to Restricted data must be limited to individuals with a documented business need and approved by a data owner.
