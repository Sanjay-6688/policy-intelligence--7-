# Data Subject Rights & Privacy Policy

## 1. Purpose
Implements data subject rights under GDPR, including the right to erasure.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personal data of EU data subjects processed by the organization.

## 3. Requirements

### 4.1 Right to Erasure
Upon a valid request, personal data of an EU data subject must be deleted within 30 days, except where the organization is subject to an overriding legal retention obligation.

### 4.2 Data Minimization
Personal data must not be retained longer than necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

### 4.3 Cross-Border Transfer
Personal data of EU data subjects may only be transferred outside the EU under an approved transfer mechanism (e.g., SCCs).
