# Acceptable Use Policy

## 1. Purpose
Sets expectations for appropriate use of corporate technology resources.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees and contractors.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Credential Hygiene
Employees should use strong passwords and are encouraged to avoid writing down credentials.

### 3.2 Secure Browsing
All web traffic should be encrypted using TLS 1.0 or higher when accessing corporate resources remotely.

### 3.3 Device Use
Corporate devices may not be used for unauthorized commercial activity.

### 3.4 Reporting
Employees must report suspected security incidents to the IT Helpdesk within 24 hours of discovery.
