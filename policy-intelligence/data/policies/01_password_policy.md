# Password Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes minimum requirements for password creation and lifecycle management across all corporate systems.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and service accounts accessing corporate IT systems.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Password Rotation
All employees must rotate passwords every 90 days for all corporate accounts, including cloud-hosted systems. Passwords that are not rotated within the 90-day window will be automatically expired.

### 3.2 Password Complexity
Passwords must be a minimum of 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numeric, and special characters.

### 3.3 Password Reuse
Employees must not reuse any of their previous 10 passwords.

## 4. Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
