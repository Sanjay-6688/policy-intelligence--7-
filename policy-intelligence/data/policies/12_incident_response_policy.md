# Incident Response Policy

## 1. Purpose
Defines procedures for responding to and containing active security incidents.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all confirmed or suspected security incidents.

## 3. Requirements

### 5.1 Logging Suspension Exception
During an active, declared security incident, the Incident Commander may authorize temporary suspension of specific logging or monitoring controls for a maximum of 4 hours if required for forensic isolation, provided the exception is documented in the incident ticket and reviewed within 24 hours by the CISO.

### 5.2 Communication
All confirmed incidents affecting Confidential or Restricted data must be reported to Legal and the DPO within 24 hours of confirmation.

### 5.3 Post-Incident Review
A post-incident review must be completed within 10 business days of incident closure.
